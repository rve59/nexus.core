# Product Vision: Nexus.core

## Vision Statement

Nexus.core is an intent-first software delivery platform where architecture intent (MGT) is the primary asset, and code realization is continuously governed by auditable agentic workflows.

## Target Users

1. Product and architecture leads managing multi-agent software delivery.
2. Developers using GAS and GAG/Copilot for staged implementation.
3. Governance and quality owners requiring traceability and audit readiness.

## Core Outcomes

1. Faster prototyping with lower architecture drift.
2. Reliable handoff from prototype to production engineering.
3. Measurable compliance between intent and implementation.
